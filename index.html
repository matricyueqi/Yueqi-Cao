Yueqi Cao’s Homepage

Welcome to my homepage!

Currently I’m a master candidate in mathematics at Beijing Institute of Technology. My advisor is Prof. Huafei Sun. My research focuses on the construction of the second fundamental form estimator on Euclidean submanifolds. A recent work can be found on arXiv.

Here is a latest version of my CV.
